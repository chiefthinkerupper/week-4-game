Ralph's Crystals Collector Game

1. The game will choose a random number at the start.

2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number generated by the game, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you unti you click on it.

5. Each time the game begins it will change the values of each crystal
